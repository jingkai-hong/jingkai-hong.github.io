I am a PhD candidate with the Behavioural Science Group at Warwick Business School in the University of Warwick, where I work with [Tim Mullett](https://www.wbs.ac.uk/about/person/tim-mullett/). I also used to be a visiting PhD student at the University of Toronto's's [Toronto Decision Neuroscience Lab](https://decisionneurolab.com/), collaborating on projects in cognitive psychology with [Cendri Hutcherson](https://www.utsc.utoronto.ca/psych/person/cendri-hutcherson). 

I am interested in understanding how people make choices and search for information by using a variety of methods, including behavioral experiments, computational modeling, and eye-tracking.
